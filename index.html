About Me

It all started back in 2021, during my final year of university. I remember scrolling through Twitter and seeing everyone talking about the Colonial Pipeline ransomware attack. That moment made me realize just how critical cybersecurity is, and that’s when my journey into this field began.

I started experimenting with tools like Kali Linux and Splunk, and I learned how to script in Python to automate tasks. I joined TryHackMe, where I ranked in the top 1% of learners, and began participating in CTF challenges. Along the way, I earned my Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate and ISC2 Certified in Cybersecurity.

Today, I’m the founder of GuardAura, a startup and the first of its kind in Somalia, where I help businesses and individuals secure their online presence. My mission is to help people stay safe in an increasingly connected world.
